Nurse

Nurse's Character traits:

  • Loyal to Juliet
  • Loving
  • Obediant to the Capulets
  • Willing to keep a secret
  • Hates disrespect to anybody
  • Tends to ramble on about nothing

Where Juliet's Nurse appears in each Act

Act 1

Scene 1

Nurse does not appear in Scene 1.

Scene 2

Nurse does not appear in Scene 2.

Scene 3

Nurse helps Lady Capulet explain to Juliet that Paris wants to marry her.

Scene 4

Nurse does not appear in this scene.

Scene 5

In this scene, Nurse is sent to fetch Juliet. When asked, Nurse tells Juliet who Romeo is, even though Romeo and Juliet had already kissed, and they didn't even know each other's names.

Act 2

Scene 1

Nurse does not appear in this scene.

Scene 2

Nurse calls Juliet's name in this scene.

Scene 3

Nurse does not appear in this scene.

Scene 4

Nurse appears is this scene, sent by Juliet. She is not respected by Mercutio and this does not fair well with her. Mercutio and Benvolio end up leaving her and Romeo alone together at the insistance of Romeo. Romeo then tells her when Juliet is to meet him at Friar Lawrences Cell. She does not like this but is willing to do it for Juliet.

Scene 5

Here Nurse tells Juliet of Romeo's message of when they are to meet together and where to get married. However, Nurse makes it difficult for Juliet to get the answer.

Scene 6

Nurse does not appear in this scene.

Act 3

Scene 1

Nurse does not appear in this scene.

Scene 2

Nurse tells Juliet of Romeo's fate in this scene. She tells Juliet that Romeo is to be banished and that Tybalt is dead. Nurse is so upset that she once again rambles on and on before answering Juliets questions.

Scene 3

Nurse has been sent by Juliet to Friar Lawrences Cell to find Romeo. Luckily for her, Romeo is there. At first, Romeo threatens to kill her but she explains how Juliet feels and calms him. This makes Romeo desire to see Juliet one last time before leaving the city.

Scene 4

Nurse does not appear in this scene.

Scene 5

Nurse warns Juliet that her parents are coming while she talks to Romeo. Juliets parents talk with Juliet and Nurse occasionally gives advice. She is on Juliet's side in this but as soon as Capulet and Lady Capulet are gone, Nurse tries to convince Juliet to let Romeo go and marry Paris. Juliet pretends to go along with it.

Act 4

Scene 1

Nurse does not appear in this scene.

Scene 2

Nurse tells Capulet that Juliet is gone to the Friar's Cell.

Scene 3

Nurse enters the room and is asked to leave by Juliet.

Scene 4

Nurse is told to waken Juliet. Nurse leaves and the scene ends.

Scene 5

Nurse finds Juliet "dead".

Act 5

Scene 1

Nurse does not appear in this scene.

Scene 2

Nurse does not appear in this scene.

Scene 3

Nurse is mentioned once in this scene.
